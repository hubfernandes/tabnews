Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Constance ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account.
  • Business Data: Information about your store, products, inventory, and team members.
  • Order Data: Details about customer orders, payments, and shipping status.
  • Customer Contact Info: Phone numbers of your customers, which are used solely to facilitate communication regarding their orders.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To manage your store's inventory and team access.
  • To facilitate communication between you and your customers via third-party messaging platforms (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • To send you technical notices, updates, and support messages.

3. Use of Platform Data (Meta/WhatsApp)

Our application integrates with the WhatsApp Business Platform. In accordance with Meta's policies:

  • We use Platform Data (specifically customer phone numbers) solely to enable you to send transactional notifications and customer service messages to your customers.
  • We do not use Platform Data for any purpose other than providing the messaging service to you.
  • We do not share Platform Data with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (i.e., sending the data to Meta/WhatsApp to deliver the message).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@constance.app.
